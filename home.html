7 steps to go Solar

01. Consultation with an energy specialist.

One of our dedicated energy specialists can walk you through the benefits of solar energy and how it can work for your specific needs. During this consultation, you will work with an expert who can assess your energy needs, evaluate your property's solar potential, and provide recommendations on the best solar solutions for you.

02. Site Assessment

One of our solar professionals will visit the site and assess the roof, shade, and other factors that may affect the installation process.

03. System Design

Based on the site assessment, the solar professional will create a custom system design that meets the customer's energy needs and local regulations.

04. Permitting and Approval

The solar installer will obtain the necessary permits and approvals from local authorities and utilities.

05. Installation

The solar installation team will mount the panels, install the electrical components, and connect the system to the grid.

06. Inspection and Commissioning

Once the system is installed, it will undergo a thorough inspection to ensure that it meets all safety and performance standards. The system will then be commissioned and connected to the electrical grid.

07. Monitoring and Maintenance

Once the system is up and running, it will be regularly monitored and maintained to ensure optimal performance and to address any issues that may arise.
